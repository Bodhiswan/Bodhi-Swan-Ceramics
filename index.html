About Bodhi Swan

Teaching and making from a grounded studio practice.

Bodhi Swan is a ceramic artist based in Brisbane, working out of Brisbane Ceramics and teaching at both Brisbane Ceramics in Milton and The Ceramic Studio in Indooroopilly.

With five years in ceramics and a practice shaped by close studio learning, he is currently focused on stoneware forms and developing a distinct glazing language through oxide washes.

Studio base Taringa and Indooroopilly, Brisbane
Teaching style Supportive small-group classes with room for one-on-one help
Current materials Stoneware, oxide washes, functional wheel-thrown forms
