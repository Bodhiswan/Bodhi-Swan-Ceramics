About Bodhi Swan

Bodhi Swan is a ceramic artist based in Brisbane, where he operates out of his studio at Brisbane Ceramics. He teaches classes at both Brisbane Ceramics in Milton and The Ceramic Studio in Indooroopilly, sharing his passion for the craft with aspiring artists.

With five years of experience in ceramics, Bodhi began his journey under the guidance of Isabell at the Logan Artists Association. He holds a Bachelor's in Arts and a Bachelor's in Economics, and is currently working with stoneware while developing his unique glazing style using oxide washes.
