As a wheel-thrown ceramics artist, I founded Brisbane Ceramics, where I also share my passion for pottery as a teacher. My practice focuses on the art of throwing on the wheel, crafting functional pieces that blend traditional techniques with contemporary design. Join our pottery classes in Brisbane to learn wheel throwing techniques in small, supportive groups.
