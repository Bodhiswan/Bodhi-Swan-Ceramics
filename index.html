About Bodhi Swan

Based in Brisbane, I create functional ceramics that blend traditional wheel-throwing techniques with contemporary minimalist design. Each piece is carefully crafted to bring beauty and mindfulness to everyday moments.

My work focuses on clean lines, natural textures, and glazes that highlight the inherent beauty of clay. I believe in creating pieces that are both beautiful and functional, designed to be used and cherished for years to come.

The Making Process

Each piece follows a careful journey from clay to finished ceramic

1

Throwing

Shaping clay on the potter's wheel with careful attention to form and proportion.

2

Drying

Slow, controlled drying to prevent cracking and prepare for the first firing.

3

Glazing

Applying glazes that will create unique colors and textures during the final firing.

4

Firing

High-temperature firing transforms clay and glaze into durable, beautiful ceramics.
