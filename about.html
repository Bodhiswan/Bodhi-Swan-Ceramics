About the artist

Bodhi
Swan

Bodhi Swan is a Brisbane-based ceramic artist whose practice moves between wheel-thrown functional work, quieter sculptural decisions, and the patient teaching of clay as a repeatable craft.

Bodhi works out of Brisbane Ceramics and teaches at both Brisbane Ceramics in Milton and The Ceramic Studio in Indooroopilly. His path into ceramics began under the guidance of Isabell at the Logan Artists Association, and over the last five years the work has gradually settled into a slower, more restrained language of stoneware, oxide washes, and useful forms.

What matters most in the studio is not speed or spectacle, but rhythm. Repetition at the wheel, attention to weight, and the quiet decisions that turn a rough form into something that can live well in the hand all sit at the center of the practice.

That same rhythm shapes the teaching. Classes are built to be supportive, practical, and grounded, with enough space for complete beginners and enough direct help for returning students to keep deepening their technique.

Artist statement
"I want the clay to keep a little of its own memory. The best pieces usually arrive when I stop trying to force a perfect outcome and let the form settle into something quieter, steadier, and more honest."
