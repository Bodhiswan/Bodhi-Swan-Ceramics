Caring for Your Ceramics

All pieces are designed for daily use and are dishwasher and microwave safe unless otherwise noted. Here are some tips to keep your ceramics beautiful for years to come.

Daily Care

Hand washing is recommended for longevity, though most pieces are dishwasher safe. Avoid extreme temperature changes.

Storage

Stack carefully with soft padding between pieces. Store in a dry place away from direct sunlight for glazed pieces.

Repairs

Small chips can often be smoothed with fine sandpaper. Contact us for advice on repairs or restoration services.
