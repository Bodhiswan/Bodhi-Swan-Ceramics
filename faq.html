Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about pottery classes, costs, and what to expect in our Brisbane studio.

What does the class fee include?

The class fee covers studio access, wheel time, tools, and expert instruction. Clay, firing, and glazing are additional costs to keep pricing flexible based on what you create.

How much does clay cost?

Clay costs approximately $3-5 per kilogram. Most beginners use 1-2kg per session, so expect around $3-10 per class. You only pay for what you use.

What are firing and glazing costs?

Firing costs are based on piece size. Small items (cups, bowls) cost $5-10 to fire. Glazing adds $3-8 per piece depending on complexity. We'll discuss options during class.

Do I need to bring anything?

Just bring yourself and clothes you don't mind getting dirty! We provide all tools, aprons, and towels. Closed-toe shoes are recommended for safety.

How long until my pieces are ready?

After throwing, pieces need to dry (1-2 weeks), then bisque fire (1 week), glaze, and final fire (1 week). Total time is typically 3-4 weeks from creation to completion.

Can complete beginners attend?

Absolutely! Our classes are designed for all skill levels. We provide guided instruction for beginners and support for more experienced potters. Everyone starts somewhere.

What if I miss a class?

Life happens! Contact us to reschedule when possible. Weekly subscription members have more flexibility with makeup classes and priority rebooking.

How do I set up weekly payments?

After attending your first class, we can set up a weekly subscription for $50/week. This gives you priority booking, a discounted rate, and consistent skill building.

Is parking available?

Yes! Free parking is available behind the studio off Coonan Street. The studio is also a short walk from Indooroopilly train station and shopping centre.

Can I book private lessons?

Yes! Private lessons are available by arrangement. Perfect for personalized instruction, flexible scheduling, or accelerated learning. Contact us to discuss your goals and schedule.

What's the class atmosphere like?

Our studio has a relaxed, supportive atmosphere. Small class sizes ensure personal attention, and we encourage creativity and experimentation in a non-judgmental environment.

Do you offer gift certificates?

Yes! Pottery classes make wonderful gifts. Contact us to arrange gift certificates for single classes, weekly sessions, or private lessons. Perfect for birthdays or special occasions.

Still Have Questions?

Can't find what you're looking for? We're here to help make your pottery journey as smooth as possible.
