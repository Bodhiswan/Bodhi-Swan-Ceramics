Frequently Asked Questions

What does the class fee include?

The $60 class fee covers studio access, wheel time, tools, and instruction. It does not include clay, firing, or glazing costs, which are additional.

How much does clay cost?

Clay costs approximately $3-5 per kilogram. Most beginners use 1-2kg per session. You only pay for the clay you use.

What are firing and glazing costs?

Firing costs are based on the size of your pieces. Small items (cups, bowls) cost $5-10 to fire. Glazing adds an additional $3-8 per piece depending on complexity.

Do I need to bring anything?

Just bring yourself and clothes you don't mind getting dirty! We provide all tools, aprons, and towels. Closed-toe shoes are recommended.

How long until my pieces are ready?

After throwing, pieces need to dry (1-2 weeks), then bisque fire (1 week), glaze, and final fire (1 week). Total time is typically 3-4 weeks.

Can complete beginners attend?

Absolutely! Our classes are designed for all skill levels. We provide guided instruction for beginners and support for more experienced potters.

What if I miss a class?

We understand life happens! Contact us to reschedule. Subscription members have more flexibility with makeup classes.

How do I set up weekly payments?

After attending your first class, we can set up a weekly subscription for $50/week. This gives you priority booking and a discounted rate.

Is parking available?

Yes! Free parking is available behind the studio off Coonan Street. The studio is also a short walk from Indooroopilly train station.

Can I book private lessons?

Yes! Private lessons are available by arrangement. Contact us at swan1995@gmail.com or call 0478 756 284 to discuss scheduling and pricing.

Still have questions?

Feel free to reach out! We're here to help make your pottery journey as smooth as possible.
