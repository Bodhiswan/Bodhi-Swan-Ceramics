What to Expect

Setup & Introduction (15 min)

Brief studio orientation, tool introduction, and wheel setup.

Guided Instruction (60 min)

Focused lessons on centering, opening, pulling, and forming techniques.

Independent Practice (90 min)

Supervised practice time with individual guidance and support.

Finishing & Cleanup (15 min)

Label pieces for drying, discuss next steps, and studio cleanup.
