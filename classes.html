Wheel throwing in Brisbane

Small classes with room to learn properly.

The classes are beginner-friendly, practical, and hands-on. There is guided instruction at the start, then time at the wheel with help as needed.

What a class feels like

Each session starts with a guided lesson on the wheel, usually covering centering, opening, pulling, and shaping. After that, there is practice time with one-on-one help.

Beginners are welcome. Returning students can keep building skills through trimming, repetition, and more specific questions.
