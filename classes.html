Wheel throwing in Brisbane

Small classes with room to learn properly.

The classes stay beginner-friendly, practical, and supported. You get guided instruction at the start, then time at the wheel with real help when something goes sideways.

What a class feels like

Each session starts with an optional guided lesson on the wheel, usually around centering, opening, pulling, and shaping. After that, the room shifts into independent practice with Bodhi circulating to help one-on-one.

Beginners are welcome. Returning students can keep building skill through trimming, refinement, and more specific technical questions.
