Class structure

Classes run 6:00–9:00 pm. You can book a single session, but they’re designed to build skills over two or more weeks.

  • Arrival & setup (10–15 min) — Brief studio intro, tools and wheel setup.
  • Guided lesson (up to 60 min) — Optional, focused on wheel basics: centering, opening, pulling and forming a simple cup or bowl.
  • Independent practice (≈120 min) — Supervised throwing and trimming at your own pace with help as needed.
  • Wrap‑up — Label your pieces for drying/firing. Glazing is scheduled at a later session.

Beginners and returners welcome. We keep class sizes small so you get hands‑on help when you want it.
