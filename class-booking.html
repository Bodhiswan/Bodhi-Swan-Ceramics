About the classes

These classes are designed for multi-week attendance. You will learn to throw items on the wheel, trim and glaze your works. Each class begins with an hour of guided instruction, followed by two hours where you can work independently to throw and trim. Materials such as clay, firing and glazing are purchased separately, either at the Indooroopilly Ceramic Studio or elsewhere.
